Frequently Asked Questions

What if I've never debated before?

No experience necessary! Our programs are designed for all levels. We start with the basics and progress at a pace suitable for beginners.

Can I attend both Thursday and Saturday sessions?

Absolutely! Many students find that attending multiple sessions accelerates their learning and provides more competitive opportunities.

Are there any additional costs?

Our core programs are affordable. Tournament participation and advanced mentorship may have additional fees, which we discuss during sign-up.

What do I need to participate?

Just a computer with internet, a microphone, and willingness to learn. We provide all debate materials and resources.
